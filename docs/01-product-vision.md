Version: 1.0

# Product Vision

Veriify.me is a modern SaaS platform that enables organizations to issue, sign, manage, and verify trusted digital documents.

The platform focuses on trust, authenticity, and simplicity.

Users can instantly verify certificates, recommendation letters, employment letters, internship letters, and signed PDF documents using a secure verification code or QR code.

Veriify.me serves educational institutions, NGOs, companies, government agencies, training providers, and professional organizations.

# Competitive Advantage

Unlike traditional e-signature platforms, Veriify.me combines document issuance, digital signatures, and instant public verification into a single platform.

Every issued credential can be independently verified without requiring a user account.
